YNCCC

Voluntary Youth initiative designed to become a hub of actions addressing the pressing sustainability issues, especially that of Climate Change in Ethiopia and beyond, as well as influencing the international discourse especially that of UNFCCC processes. YNCCC was initiated in the year 2014 to support youth climate actions across Ethiopia and beyond.
