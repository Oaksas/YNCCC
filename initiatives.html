Pick Paper

Pick Papers is a mobile application that aims to break the barrier between the Paper recyclers and the recyclable papers. The main feature of the app is that users can request paper pickup and the collectors will get a location-tagged picture and approximate size of the paper to be collected. Then Collectors can use the app to contact the users and pick the paper. The next step will be to deliver it to the paper recycling companies. The app is built using Flutter programming language which makes it available for both ios and Android phones. For the time being its operating in Addis Ababa only.
